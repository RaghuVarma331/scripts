### How to use?

    * mkdir keys
    * cd keys
    * wget https://github.com/RaghuVarma331/scripts/raw/master/private-keys-Generator/keys.sh
    * chmod a+x keys.sh
    * nano keys.sh
    * add your details at top
    Example- 
            # your country
            C=IN

            # Your state
            ST=Telangana

            # Your Location
            L=Hyderabad

           # Your Email
